Volunteer Opportunities

We are so happy that you want to help out! To volunteer at the Crighead Forst Park there are two possible ways to get involved.

The first way to join is by signing up to do volunteer work through the Jonesboro Parks and Recreation website. You can do this by:

  • First click here and fill out the form.
  • When selecting where you want to volunteer, choose the Craighead Forest Park.
  • wait for the Jonesboro staff contact you about upcoming volunteer opportunities.
  • And you're done!

You can also volunteer by joining the Craighead Nature Center where you can either become a normal volunteer, or you can offer any of your experiences in various fields like hunting, or fishing and become a cetticied instructor.

You can do this by:

  • First click here and create your own account.
  • Depending on your age you can choose between two you can choose between AGFC Youth Volunteer (18 < years old) or AGFC Volunteer (18> years old). Choose the one that fits your age.
  • Fill out the application and wait for the staff to approve your volunteer aplication, you can access all of the events and certifications.
