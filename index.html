Welcome to the Craighead Forest Park

Welcome to the Craighead Forest Park! Home to beautiful scenery, exciting activities, and unforgettable experiences. Whether you're here for a day of fishing, a weekend of camping, or just to explore the great outdoors, we have something for everyone. Located at 4910 S Culberhouse Rd, Jonesboro, AR 72404, our park offers a variety of amenities including picnic areas, hiking trails, and playgrounds. As well as campsites and fishing spots
