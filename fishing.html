Fishing at Craighead Forest Lake is a great pastime for visitors of all ages. The Arkansas Game and Fish Commission stocks the lake with:

  • Brass
  • Bream
  • Crappie
  • Tilapia

You can fish from the docks and jetties

Or

Launch boats to fish on the lake!

  • Only allowed to fish during daylight hours.
  • 5 mph speed limit
  • "No wake" call enforced all the time

Family Fishing Area

The Family Fishing Area is near pavillion 5, being handicapped accessible. Allowing for all families to spend time together!

The Arkansas Game and Fish Commission stocks the lake with:

  • Brass
  • Bream
  • Catfish
  • Tilapia(summer)
  • Trout (winter)
