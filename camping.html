Camping

The Craighead Forest Park Campground lets bisitors enjoy the views that this park has to offer with its rustic athomosphere. Being allowed all year round, the camp offers many commodities across the facilities.

The Campground offers:

  • 5 Tents at the edge of the lake

The park also offers RV 36 sites on the campground, each having:

  • Water
  • 30 or 50 amp electrical hookup
  • Access to a personal table, patio, fire ring and grill
  • Pets on a leash are welcomed
  • Handicap access
