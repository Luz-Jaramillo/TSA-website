Fishing

Warning

Everyone over the age of 16 must have a valid fishing license.

For more information on fhishing licenses click here

Fishing at the Craightead Forest Lake is a great pastime for visitors of all ages.

The Arakansas Game and Fish Comission stacks the lake with:

  • Bluegill
  • Largemouth Bass
  • Smallmouth Bass
  • Channel Catfish
    • You can fish at the docks and jetties or you can launch boats on the lake.

    The family Fishing Area is near Pavilion 5, and is handicapped accesible, allowing all families to spend time together no matte what.

    The Arkansas Game and Fish Commission stocks the lake with:

    • Bass
    • Bream
    • Tilapia (in summer)
    • Catfish
    • Trout
